ACME Corp AI Services Agreement
Contract ID: AC-2026-0142
Effective Date: January 15, 2026

PARTIES
This agreement is entered between Assurant, Inc. ("Provider") and ACME Corp ("Customer")
for the deployment of AI-powered customer support services.

SECTION 1 — SERVICE DESCRIPTION
Provider shall deploy an AI-powered customer support assistant for ACME Corp's
European customer base. The assistant will handle customer inquiries related to
product returns, warranty claims, and general product questions.

SECTION 2 — DATA HANDLING AND PRIVACY
2.1 All customer data must be processed within the European Union in compliance
with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).
2.2 No customer PII (Personally Identifiable Information) shall be transmitted to
servers located outside the EU.
2.3 Data retention shall not exceed 90 days for conversation logs.
2.4 Customer data shall be encrypted at rest and in transit.

SECTION 3 — APPROVED AI PROVIDERS
3.1 The Customer approves the use of the following AI model providers:
    - Anthropic (Claude model family)
    - OpenAI (GPT model family)
3.2 The use of DeepSeek, Baidu, or any China-based AI provider is strictly
prohibited under this agreement.
3.3 Provider may not route requests to any model provider not explicitly
listed in Section 3.1 without prior written approval.

SECTION 4 — PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Support responses must be delivered within 1 second (1000ms) for standard
customer queries.
4.2 The system should prioritize response speed over reasoning depth for
routine inquiries (e.g., FAQ responses, order status checks).
4.3 Complex inquiries requiring detailed analysis may have extended response
times up to 5 seconds.
4.4 System uptime SLA: 99.5%

SECTION 5 — COST AND BILLING
5.1 ACME Corp operates on a cost-sensitive basis for AI services.
5.2 The service should minimize inference costs while maintaining acceptable
quality for customer interactions.
5.3 Monthly AI inference budget cap: $5,000
5.4 Provider shall optimize model selection to reduce unnecessary premium
model usage.

SECTION 6 — USE CASE SPECIFICATIONS
6.1 Primary use case: Automated customer support chatbot
6.2 Expected volume: approximately 10,000 queries per day
6.3 Estimated query distribution:
    - Simple queries (FAQ, status checks): 65%
    - Medium queries (returns, basic troubleshooting): 30%
    - Complex queries (escalations, multi-issue resolution): 5%
6.4 Supported languages: English, French, German
